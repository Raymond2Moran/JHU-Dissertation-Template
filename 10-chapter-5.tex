\section{Synthesis}

The results connect three layers of evidence:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Protein prediction performance comparisons.
    \item Valid uncertainty quantification with limited labels.
    \item Prospective planning via sample-size analysis.
\end{enumerate}

Taken together, they support a practical thesis message: efficient, uncertainty-aware biological prediction is achievable when strong surrogates are paired with principled prediction-powered inference.
